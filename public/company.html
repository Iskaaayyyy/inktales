Company Introduction

Introduction of our company.

InkVerse is a creative company built to manage writing and art studio operations. The company focuses on helping storytellers organize their ideas, develop written works, and connect their stories with visual formats such as comics, manga-inspired pages, and animated story previews.

The company was created to support modern digital storytelling by combining writing, visual creativity, publishing tools, and reader engagement in one organized ecosystem. Through its product, InkTales, InkVerse provides a platform where users can write, read, publish, earn, and transform stories into visual outputs.

Introduction of InkTales

The flagship mobile app of InkVerse.

InkTales is the main product of InkVerse. It is designed as a mobile-first writing and reading platform where one account can act as both reader and writer.

InkTales allows users to create stories, save drafts, publish works, read stories from other creators, top up coins, unlock paid stories, tip writers, and apply for monetization after meeting platform requirements.

One of its main features is story conversion. When a writer publishes a story, the system can convert the written content into manga-style pages, colored comic pages, or animated story preview outputs. This makes the platform useful not only for reading and writing, but also for visual storytelling.
